Skiing

Skiing is the use of skis to glide the snow.

Skiing is a recreational activity as well as a competitive sport. The skier attaches long skis, also called runners to specially designed boots and uses them to travel on top of snow

Simplifying the sport, all you have to do is skid on the snow while balancing your body. But, make sure you have proper gear and training available so as to avoid injuries. It is a winter sport with some spots in the world-famous for the experience. Gulmarg in Kashmir is amongst the top places. Make sure to ski when you visit Kashmir.
Requirements:
Age- Above 5 years
Weight- 14 kgs or Above
Cost- 950Rs


  • Discover the perfect blend of adventure and natural beauty: Ski in Gulmarg, Kashmir!

  • Instructor: Mr.Parwez Ahmed

Rafting

Rafting, also known as whitewater rafting, is an exhilarating outdoor activity that involves navigating down rivers or other bodies of water in an inflatable raft. It is typically done in groups and requires teamwork and coordination to maneuver through rapids and turbulent water.

Distance and Duration: Kali River in Dandeli offers a rafting stretch of up to 12 kms. The stretch of river is covered with dense forests, offers several grade 2 (simpler to negotiate) and grade 3 (needs a bit more effort) rapids making the rafting experience exhilarating, adventurous and memorable. The 12 km rafting excursion lasts 3 to 4 hours end to end, including transportation from the resort to the starting point.
Requirements:
Age - 12 years
Cost- Rs950-Rs2500

Hang Gliding

Love the feeling of fresh air hitting you on the face? Well, with hang gliding you can feel the air above a few hundred feet, conquer the skies gliding on them and witness mesmeric views from up above the world. You can do hang gliding in Mumbai also.
Hang gliding is an exhilarating and thrilling air sport that involves flying a lightweight, unpowered aircraft known as a hang glider. The pilot hangs below a fabric wing, controlling the aircraft's direction and speed by shifting their body weight.
Exploring the hills from a vantage point in the sky may just be the best way to spend your vacation in Ooty. A single flight may cost anywhere between Rs 1,200 to 2,500 depending on the duration.

Requirements:
Age- 13 years
Cost - Rs1200-Rs2500
